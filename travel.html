HOSTING COUNTRY

ICACIE-2023 will be organized at Universite Des Mascareignes, a public university In Mauritius. Mauritius is an island nation in the Indian Ocean about 2,000 kilometres (1,100 nautical miles) off the southeast coast of the African continent, east of Madagascar. The environment in Mauritius is typically tropical in the coastal regions with forests in the mountainous areas and consists of many viewpoints and activities which can be done in Mauritius only.
